Piano Studio of Anna Carter

SLC & Provo, UT

My name is Anna Carter and I have been playing piano since age 8. I absolutely love it and love teaching others how to play. I am currently attending school at Brigham Young University and am in the Finance program. I got back from my mission for The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints in November of 2021. I grew up on the East Coast and then spent the second half of my life in Northern California. My family then moved to Salt Lake while I was on my mission. I taught piano for four years in high school and loved every second of it.

In order to teach, I come to your house/wherever a piano is available to you. Click this link to see details on purchasing a piano.
